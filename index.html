Who we are

Zkip Labs was founded to solve a simple but expensive problem: cloud bills that grow faster than value delivered. We are a small, focused engineering team that builds cloud-native tools combining static analysis, live API inspection, and pricing intelligence to give you a clear, honest picture of your cloud spend — and exactly what to do about it.

🎯

Our mission

Democratize cloud cost intelligence. Whether you are a three-person startup or a large enterprise, you deserve the same visibility into waste and the same levers to fix it — without hiring a dedicated FinOps team.

⚡

How we work

We connect read-only to your cloud accounts, run deep analysis across compute, storage, networking, and managed services, then surface ranked recommendations with dollar-value impact so you always know what to tackle first.

🏗️

For small businesses

Cloud costs are the fastest-growing line item for growing teams. Zkip Labs finds the low-hanging fruit — unused snapshots, over-provisioned databases, abandoned data transfers — so you can redirect that budget toward growth.

🏭

For enterprises

At scale, even small inefficiencies compound into millions of dollars. Our tooling integrates with your existing CMDB and tagging strategy to deliver portfolio-level savings analysis across accounts, regions, and teams.

🔐

Security & trust

We request only read-only IAM permissions and never store raw resource data beyond the analysis session. Your infrastructure stays yours. We operate under a least-privilege principle by default — no agents, no ongoing access unless you explicitly grant it.
